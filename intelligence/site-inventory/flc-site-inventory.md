## 3. ABOUT FLC

### Institutional Identity
- **Type:** Public liberal arts college
- **Location:** 1000 Rim Drive, Durango, CO 81301
- **Elevation:** 7,600 ft (mesa overlooking Durango)
- **Region:** Four Corners, Southwest Colorado

### Leadership
- **President:** Dr. Heather J. Shotton (11th President, first Native American)
- **Governance:** 9-member Board of Trustees
  - 7 Governor-appointed
  - 1 nonvoting student member
  - 1 nonvoting faculty member

### History
Evolution pathway:
1. Military outpost (Fort Lewis)
2. Indian boarding school
3. Rural high school
4. Public liberal arts college (current)

### Unique Differentiators
- Only public liberal arts college in the Four Corners
- "Learn by doing" experiential education model
- Deep connection to Southwest Colorado landscapes
- 300+ days of sunshine
- Proximity to 300 miles of trails
- Historic "Old Fort" campus area with sustainable farming

### Key Initiatives

|------------|-------------|
| Village Aid Project | Engineering students design global water/sanitation infrastructure |
| Water in the West | Multidisciplinary regional water resource research |
| Hawk Tank | Business competition with $5,000 startup funding |

## 11. OBSERVATIONS & PATTERNS

### Strengths
1. Clear primary navigation structure
2. Strong outdoor/adventure brand positioning
3. Transparent tuition and aid information
4. Diversity and inclusion prominently featured
5. Student testimonials add authenticity

### Opportunities for SKYRIDGE
1. **Information Architecture:** Content is scattered across many subdomains and portals
2. **Audience Routing:** No clear "I am a..." entry point for different stakeholders
3. **Personalization:** Generic experience regardless of visitor type
4. **Visual Cohesion:** Multiple visual styles across sections
5. **Mobile Experience:** Desktop-optimized patterns
6. **Search/Discovery:** Relies on traditional nav vs. intelligent discovery
7. **Storytelling:** Facts presented without narrative flow
8. **Integration:** Separate systems (Canvas, Workday, etc.) feel disconnected

### Key Differentiators to Amplify
1. Only public liberal arts college in Four Corners
2. 95% undergraduate research participation
3. Native American heritage and tuition waiver
4. Adventure Education unique program
5. Location (elevation, trails, ski access)
6. Small class sizes (19 avg, 100% under 50)
7. First Native American president
